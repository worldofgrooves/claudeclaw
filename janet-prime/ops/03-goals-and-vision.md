# Goals and Vision

## Long-Term Vision

Denver is building a creative ecosystem with three interconnected pillars:

1. **World of Grooves** — globally recognized fine art practice; large-scale installations in luxury venues and hotels; collector relationships; IP and product extensions
2. **Plume Creative** — award-winning boutique brand studio; recognized name in luxury creative direction; $75K+ project engagements
3. **Creative IP** — narrative worlds, characters, and product lines derived from original artwork (Groove Dwellers, Le Freq, etc.)

The long-term trajectory is for **World of Grooves to become the primary practice**, with Plume Creative generating bridge revenue in the interim.
