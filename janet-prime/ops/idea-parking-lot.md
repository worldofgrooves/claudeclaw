# Idea Parking Lot

Ideas live here instead of immediately becoming active projects. This preserves creativity without overwhelming execution capacity.

Ideas may move to `creative-pipeline.md` when timing, resources, and strategic alignment are right.

---

## Format

Each idea should include:
- **Idea:** Brief description
- **Why interesting:** Strategic or creative potential
- **What it needs:** Resources, timing, or dependencies
- **Status:** Parked / Revisit [date] / Ready to develop

---

## Parked Ideas

### AR / NFC Artwork Integration
- **Idea:** Embed NFC tags or AR triggers in World of Grooves artwork so viewers can use their phone to see the artwork "come to life" — records spin, reference imagery surfaces
- **Why interesting:** Creates interactive, shareable experiences. Differentiates WoG in luxury venue context.
- **What it needs:** A completed piece to prototype with. App or platform for AR delivery. Developer partner.
- **Status:** Parked — revisit after Hard Rock outreach establishes placement opportunities

### QR Code / Wayfinding in Artwork
- **Idea:** Embed QR codes into finished pieces linking to an experience — location, reference imagery, creation process video
- **Why interesting:** Deepens collector and viewer experience. Ongoing brand touchpoint after sale.
- **What it needs:** Simple web build or link destination per piece
- **Status:** Parked — low effort when the time is right

### Animated Watercolor for Social Media (Matices)
- **Idea:** Use Higgsfield / Kling 3.0 to create animated versions of Matices brand watercolor illustrations for social media
- **Why interesting:** Would distinguish Matices social presence from every other restaurant.
- **What it needs:** Amié's approval and a clear brief. Could be a small add-on project.
- **Status:** Parked — worth proposing as a separate engagement after signage closes

### Artifact App — NFC Authentication Platform
- **Idea:** NFC-based authentication and ownership verification platform for musicians and artists; also being explored as a "superfan wearable art platform" for World of Grooves
- **Why interesting:** Sits at intersection of Denver's art practice, music background, and technology interest; potential for passive/scalable revenue
- **Reference model:** Ixkio (studied for NFC authentication and content-gating approach)
- **What it needs:** Technical development partner, clearer product definition, funding or partnership model, pilot artists
- **Status:** Early research — Denver is actively gathering information and having conversations with potential pilot artists
