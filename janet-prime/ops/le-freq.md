# Le Freq

## What It Is

Le Freq is a fashion and wearable art concept connected to Denver's visual design language — translating the geometric, frequency-inspired, and music-culture-rooted elements of his artwork into wearable form.

---

## Concept Areas

- Apparel (T-shirts, jackets, limited-edition drops)
- Accessories
- Wearable art and statement pieces
- Creative collaborations with fashion designers or brands
- Possible connection to Groove Dwellers narrative world

---

## Strategic Value

- Extends Denver's creative IP into a consumer product category
- Music and vinyl culture overlap strongly with streetwear and fashion markets
- Could function as a marketing channel for World of Grooves (wearers become brand ambassadors)
- Limited-edition drops could support collector relationships

---

## Current Status

Early concept — **parked**, not in active development.

**What it needs before developing:**
- Defined aesthetic direction (how does this look different from generic "art on a shirt"?)
- Production partner or platform (print-on-demand vs. limited-run manufacturer)
- Clear connection to World of Grooves brand — does Le Freq live under WoG, or is it its own brand?
- Proof-of-concept piece
