# Content System & Audience Growth

## Strategic Purpose

Growing Denver's online audience and authority is a core business development strategy — not a vanity metric.

A strong, recognizable presence directly supports:

- Inbound commission inquiries from collectors and private buyers
- Gallery and venue interest (credibility signal before outreach)
- Institutional partnerships with brands like Hard Rock and luxury hospitality groups
- Plume Creative positioning as a premium studio with a recognizable creative director

Content creation is a **strategic visibility engine**, not just marketing.

Audience growth is treated as a **long-term asset**, similar to a portfolio or collector network.  
The objective is sustained credibility and visibility — not short-term viral attention.

---

## Audience Growth Goals

- Increase social media engagement across Instagram and TikTok
- Build a recognizable presence within fine art, music culture, and creative communities
- Attract collectors, galleries, and major venue partnerships through demonstrated authority
- Grow the collector pipeline so commissions are driven by inbound interest, not outbound prospecting alone

# Content Capture Workflow

## Purpose

Denver captures content throughout the artwork creation process to generate multiple marketing assets from each completed piece.

The goal is to **maximize marketing value while keeping capture simple and integrated into the creative workflow**, not requiring a separate production effort.

---

## Capture Workflow

Each artwork (internally referred to as an **RP — completed piece**) follows a consistent capture sequence.

### During creation

1. **Time-lapse recording**  
   Continuous capture of the assembly process.

2. **Real-time footage**  
   Capture key moments such as:
   - placing an important piece
   - intricate fabrication work
   - revealing recognizable elements
   - satisfying assembly moments

---

### After completion

3. **Hang the artwork on the studio wall**

4. **Capture all final content in a single session**  
   Lighting is already configured and consistent.

Capture includes:

- close-up detail shots
- slow-motion or cinematic clips
- full-frame shots of the completed artwork
- portfolio-quality photographs
- footage suitable for social media or YouTube

This batching approach minimizes production effort while maximizing content output.

---

## Content Output Target

Each completed artwork should produce **a minimum of five social media posts**.

| Content Type | Format | Platform |
|---|---|---|
| Time-lapse assembly | Short video | Instagram Reels, TikTok |
| Fabrication moment | Short clip | Instagram Reels, TikTok |
| Detail shots | Photo or slow-motion clip | Instagram, TikTok |
| Final reveal | Video or photo | Instagram, TikTok, YouTube |
| Studio wall display | Photo or cinematic clip | Instagram, portfolio |

Additional extractable content:

- behind-the-scenes captions
- YouTube long-form process video
- YouTube Shorts
- portfolio photography
- press and outreach images
- gallery or venue submission assets

Janet may assist in maintaining **1–2 months of scheduled content** when possible.

---

## Voice and Tone

Content should reflect Denver's identity as a **fine artist and creative director**, not a generic social media content creator.

Posts should feel:

- intentional
- confident
- visually elevated
- aligned with gallery-level presentation

Caption approach:

- lead with the work, not the process
- process details should support the narrative
- avoid overly casual or trend-driven tone
- World of Grooves content should feel appropriate alongside luxury brand or gallery content

---

## Content Multiplication Principle

Every completed artwork should generate **multiple marketing assets**.

Target extraction from each piece:

- **5+ short-form posts**
- **1 optional long-form YouTube video**
- **3–5 portfolio photographs**
- **1–2 outreach-ready images** for galleries, venues, or press

The objective is to maximize the marketing value of each artwork without requiring constant new production.
