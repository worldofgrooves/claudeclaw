# Groove Dwellers

## What It Is

Groove Dwellers is a narrative creative IP concept imagining creatures that live within the grooves of vinyl records.

It exists at the intersection of Denver's fine art practice, his love of music culture, and his interest in world-building and storytelling.

---

## Concept

The core idea: the physical grooves of a vinyl record are home to tiny, unseen inhabitants — the Groove Dwellers. When the needle drops and music plays, their world comes alive.

Potential extensions:
- Illustrated characters and narrative storytelling
- Animation and short-form video
- Immersive or interactive installations
- Licensing and creative IP
- Apparel and product design (connection to Le Freq)
- Children's books or adult graphic novels

---

## Strategic Value

- Creates a **proprietary narrative world** attached to Denver's core medium
- Differentiates World of Grooves from other vinyl artists — it's not just visual, it's a universe
- IP development could generate licensing revenue independent of commission work
- Strong alignment with music culture, nostalgia, and collector markets
