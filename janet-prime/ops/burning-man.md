# Burning Man Projects

## Overview

Denver creates installation artwork connected to the Burning Man community — large-scale sculpture, lighting, and immersive environments.

Strategic purposes:
- Platform for large-scale artistic experimentation
- Profile-building within the art and installation community
- Proof of creative ambition and technical capability that transfers to commercial work
- Grant funding as validation of artistic merit

---

## Echo of Emergence

**The piece:** A 6.5-foot butterfly sculpture constructed from vinyl records.

**Recognition:** 2025 Burning Man **Honoraria Grant** recipient — a competitive, jury-selected grant.

**Significance:**
- Establishes Denver's credibility as a serious installation artist
- Burning Man Honoraria is internationally recognized in the art world
- Proof point used in client conversations (e.g., IVGID call with Paul)
- Sets the pricing floor for major World of Grooves commissions

**Price floor:** Established at a significant figure. Baseline for all major sculpture commissions. Do not negotiate below this.

## Strategic Role of Burning Man Work

Burning Man projects give Denver:
- Access to a global community of artists, collectors, and curators
- Experimental space to develop large-scale fabrication skills
- A narrative that bridges fine art and commercial creative work
- Media and documentation that supports portfolio and press outreach
